Working with PDFs often creates the same kinds of friction:

- the more papers and documents you collect, the harder they are to find
- you want to highlight and annotate while reading
- you want to ask AI questions, but still verify the source passages
- management, viewing, search, and AI chat are split across separate tools

AskPDF is a PDF workflow SaaS built to solve those problems in one place. It is designed so that PDF management, viewing, search, and AI chat can happen in a single screen.

## What is AskPDF?

AskPDF is a service for handling PDFs more efficiently. It is not just a place to store files. It brings together the full flow of reading, searching, asking questions, and organizing information inside one product.

![AskPDF main screen with PDF list, viewer, and AI chat in one layout](/articles/pdf-management-ai-chat/hero.jpg)

It can be used by students and researchers reading papers, and also for business use cases such as contracts, proposals, and internal manuals.

## What problems does AskPDF solve?

AskPDF is built to unify PDF work into one continuous workflow. Instead of moving between different tools to find a file, open it, inspect the right section, ask questions, and verify the answer, you can do those tasks in the same interface.

That reduction in tool switching is one of its biggest practical advantages.

## What you can do with AskPDF

### 1. Manage PDFs in one place

You can manage uploaded PDFs in a single list. This makes it easier to keep documents from becoming scattered across multiple places.

### 2. Work while reading

You can open a PDF in the central viewer and add annotations such as highlights and underlines while you read. That makes it easier to organize information in a way you can review later.

### 3. Switch across multiple PDFs with tabs

You can switch between multiple PDFs using tabs. This is useful when you need to compare documents or work with more than one file in parallel.

### 4. Jump to annotated sections quickly

You can move directly to annotated sections from the minimap beside the viewer. Even in long PDFs, it becomes easier to keep track of important passages.

![Minimap showing annotation markers inside the PDF viewer](/articles/pdf-management-ai-chat/minimap.jpg)

### 5. Chat with AI about a PDF

You can ask AI questions about the content of a PDF. AskPDF is designed to show the referenced passages behind answers, so you can verify the evidence yourself.

It is also possible to keep multiple chats for the same PDF, which helps separate topics by task or purpose.

### 6. Search inside PDF content

You can search not only by file name, but also by the contents of the PDF itself. This is useful when you need to find specific text or keywords quickly across many files.

### 7. Use OCR for image-based PDFs

AskPDF supports OCR, so it works not only with text PDFs but also with image-based PDFs. That means search, chat, and highlighting can still work on scanned materials and image-heavy documents.

![AskPDF OCR view showing handwritten PDF content and chat](/articles/pdf-management-ai-chat/askpdf-ocr.jpg)

This is especially valuable for handwritten or scan-based documents that are otherwise difficult to use.

### 8. Speed up work with shortcuts

Many actions are available through keyboard shortcuts, so the workflow gets faster as you become familiar with it.

### 9. Use a simple interface

AskPDF is designed to make important functions easy to reach through a simple and direct UI.

## What makes AskPDF different?

The main characteristic of AskPDF is that PDF management and AI usage are not split apart. Finding a PDF, reading it, asking questions, and verifying answers can happen in the same product.

It is also designed around traceability. The goal is not only convenience, but also confidence when checking the basis of AI answers.

## Who is it for?

- students and researchers who read papers continuously
- business users who work with contracts, proposals, and manuals
- people who store many PDFs and need to find the right content quickly
- people who want AI assistance but also need to confirm the source passages

## Before using AskPDF

AskPDF makes AI-assisted PDF work more convenient, but AI answers are not always perfect. For important decisions or citations, checking the original text is still recommended.
